Our Technology

We combine purpose-driven engineering, indigenous innovation, and cutting-edge drone technology to create bespoke UAV solutions that drive your operational success.

Aeronica delivers integrated drone solutions — robust hardware, smart software, and reliable services — tailored to user needs. Supported end-to-end, we help organizations confidently start and scale drone programs, emphasizing reliability, compliance, and Indian manufacturing excellence.

How We Work

01

Consult

We begin with a deep understanding of your operational needs, site conditions, and compliance requirements to define the optimal drone solution.

02

Our certified pilots deploy advanced UAVs with RTK, LiDAR, and multispectral sensors to capture high-fidelity data across your project area.

04

Deliver

You receive comprehensive reports, 3D models, and interactive dashboards with ongoing support to ensure your project's success.
